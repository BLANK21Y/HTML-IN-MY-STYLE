About HTML in My Style

We're on a mission to make learning HTML accessible, interactive, and enjoyable for everyone.

Our Story

HTML in My Style was born out of a simple idea: learning to code should be accessible, engaging, and fun for everyone. We noticed that many coding platforms were either too complex for beginners or too basic for those wanting to advance their skills.

Founded in 2023 by a team of passionate web developers and educators, our platform combines interactive learning experiences with practical projects. We focus on teaching not just the syntax of HTML, but also best practices, accessibility considerations, and modern web development techniques.

Today, our community has grown to over 10,000 learners worldwide, from complete beginners to professional developers refreshing their skills.

Our Mission

We believe that coding literacy is becoming increasingly important in today's digital world. Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills to create for the web, regardless of their background or prior experience.

We're committed to breaking down barriers to learning by providing:

  • Free, high-quality educational content
  • Interactive coding exercises with immediate feedback
  • A supportive community where questions are encouraged
  • Real-world projects that build practical skills
  • Accessibility features for learners of all abilities

By focusing on these core principles, we aim to create a learning experience that's not just educational, but truly transformative.
