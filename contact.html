Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How do I get started with HTML in My Style?

Getting started is easy! Simply create a free account, and you'll have immediate access to our beginner HTML courses. We recommend starting with our "HTML Fundamentals" course if you're new to coding. Each lesson builds upon the previous one, and you can progress at your own pace.

Are the courses free or paid?

We offer both free and premium content. Our basic courses and resources are completely free, allowing you to learn the fundamentals of HTML without any cost. For more advanced courses, projects, and personalized feedback, we offer affordable premium plans starting at $9.99 per month.

Do I need any prior coding experience?

No prior coding experience is necessary! Our courses are designed to be accessible to complete beginners. We start with the very basics and gradually introduce more complex concepts as you progress. Our interactive learning approach makes it easy to understand even if you've never written a line of code before.

How long does it take to learn HTML?

The time it takes to learn HTML varies depending on your learning pace and dedication. On average, you can grasp the basics of HTML in about 2-3 weeks with consistent practice. To become proficient and comfortable building websites, most students spend 2-3 months working through our curriculum and completing projects.

Can I receive a certificate after completing courses?

Yes! Upon completing a course, you'll receive a digital certificate that you can add to your LinkedIn profile or include in your resume. Our certificates are recognized by many employers and demonstrate your commitment to developing your web development skills.
